04 — Ventures

We invest in what we create.

We don't just work for creativity — we invest in it. Cuéntamelo Cantando, our singer-songwriter contest, is where that starts: each season's winner signs an artistic development contract with Mantra — real investment, real production, a real stake in what happens next. Season one's winner is already a working artist, with a released single and a place on PULZ Music. That's the difference between a company that provides services and one that builds a lasting body of work.

05 — Publishing

We publish songwriters.

Soul Writing is Mantra's publishing arm, built on a simple belief: a song starts with someone who felt something and had to write it down. We manage rights fairly, open doors to real recording opportunities, and build marketing support around the songs and the people who write them. The craft comes first; the business exists to protect it.
